About Me

I work for Disney Floral. I deliver flower arrangements to guests staying at Disney Resorts as well as setting up in-room celebrations for birthdays, anniversaries, or any other celebration the guest could want. I currently attending a coding bootcamp to improve my skills and change my career path. I moved to Windermere from Chicagoland about two years ago. I have to say I do love it here and do not miss the snow. I do however, miss the Chicago Style Pizza. I am a huge Elvis Presley fan and have been to Graceland about 5 times.
